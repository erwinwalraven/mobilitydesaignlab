Postdoc vacancies will be made available soon

We are currently in the process of preparing two postdoc vacancies at Delft University of Technology, which will be made available soon. We expect to finalize the hiring process in the first months of 2026.

Mobility DesAIgn Lab kickoff in Amsterdam

Our lab has officially started with a kickoff with all consortium partners, hosted by the AMS Institute. All work packages discussed research directions and identified first steps for the use cases.

Four projects launched in NGF-Call AiNed ELSA Labs

NWO has funded four projects in the NGF call AiNed ELSA Labs. Mobility DesAIgn Lab received funding to study the ethical, legal, societal, and economic implications of using prescriptive digital twins for mobility system design.
